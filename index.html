Our Services

Admin Dashboard

The admin dashboard allows administrators to manage the entire school scheduling system. Administrators can:

  • Create and manage staff accounts
  • Define programs and courses
  • Assign staff to courses
  • View and manage schedules

Staff Dashboard

The staff dashboard provides tools for teachers and instructors to manage their schedules and classes. Staff members can:

  • Create and manage schedules
  • Take attendance
  • Update course content
  • Communicate with students

Student Dashboard

The student dashboard offers students access to their courses, tasks, and schedules. Students can:

  • View their course schedule
  • Access course materials and assignments
  • Submit assignments
  • View grades and progress
